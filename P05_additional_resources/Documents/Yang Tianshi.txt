Yang Tianshi (Chinese: 杨天石; pinyin: Yáng Tiānshí; born 15 February 1936) is a Chinese historian who is a professor at the Graduate School of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences. He is a member of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences and the Central Research Institute of Culture and History. He has been hailed as first person from the Chinese mainland to study Chiang Kai-shek.
