Christian German School Chiang Mai (German: Christliche Deutsche Schule Chiang Mai, CDSC; Thai: โรงเรียนคริสเตียนเยอรมันเชียงใหม่, RTGS: Rong Rian Khrittian Yoeraman Chiang Mai) is a German international school in Chiang Mai, Thailand.
It serves from nursery to secondary stage II.The current nursery building opened in June 2013.
