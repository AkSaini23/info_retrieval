Mong Ko (Burmese: မုန်းကိုးမြို့; Chinese: 勐古; pinyin: Měng gǔ), sometimes spelled Mongko or Monekoe and also known as Man Kan, Man Guo and Panglong, is a town in Mu Se Township, Mu Se District, northern Shan State. 
Like many towns in the region, Mong Ko is known to be a hotspot for drug production and trade.
