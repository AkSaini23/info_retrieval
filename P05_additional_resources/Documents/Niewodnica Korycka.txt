Niewodnica Korycka [ɲevɔdˈnit͡sa kɔˈrɨt͡ska] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Turośń Kościelna, within Białystok County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north of Turośń Kościelna and 7 km (4 mi) south-west of the regional capital Białystok.
The village has a population of 120.
