Serhiy Vitaliyovych Nadal (Ukrainian: Сергій Віталійович Надал; born 1 January 1975) is a Ukrainian politician. He is the mayor of Ternopil and head of the Ternopil United territorial community in western Ukraine. A member of the party All-Ukrainian Union "Svoboda", Nadal has been mayor since 2010. Nadal election in 2010 was the first mayoral election won by "Svoboda".
