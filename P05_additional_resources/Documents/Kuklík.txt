Kuklík is a village and municipality (obec) in Žďár nad Sázavou District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 7.84 square kilometres (3.03 sq mi), and has a population of 179 (as at 28 August 2006).
Kuklík lies approximately 15 kilometres (9 mi) north-east of Žďár nad Sázavou, 46 km (29 mi) north-east of Jihlava, and 132 km (82 mi) south-east of Prague.
