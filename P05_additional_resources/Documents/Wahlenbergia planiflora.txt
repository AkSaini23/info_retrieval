Wahlenbergia planiflora, commonly known as flat bluebell, is a small herbaceous plant in the family Campanulaceae native to eastern Australia.
The tufted perennial herb typically grows to a height of 0.12 to 0.70 metres (0 to 2 ft). It blooms throughout the year producing blue-yellow-white flowers.The species is found in New South Wales, Victoria and Queensland.
There are two known subspecies:

Wahlenbergia planiflora subsp. longipila
