Poznowice [pɔznɔˈvit͡sɛ] (German: Posnowitz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Izbicko, within Strzelce County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south of Izbicko, 10 km (6 mi) west of Strzelce Opolskie, and 23 km (14 mi) south-east of the regional capital Opole.
