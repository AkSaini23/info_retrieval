Nagasaki Baseball Stadium (長崎県営野球場, Nagasaki Ken'ei Yakyūjō, Nagasaki Big N Stadium) is a baseball stadium in the city of Nagasaki, Japan. The stadium was built in 1997 and has an all-seated capacity of 25,000. The Nagasaki Saints played some home games there. It is located about 320m from the epicenter of the nuclear bombing of Nagasaki.
